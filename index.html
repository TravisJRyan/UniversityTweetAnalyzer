SEC University Tweet Analyzer

Travis Ryan, WKU Honors Thesis

Keywords Used

Sports: game, sports, ball, soccer, lacrosse, field hockey, bowling, tennis, hockey, rugby, athletics, athlete, stadium, field, mascot
Academics: honors, research, paper, study, academic, science, class, intern

Methodology

I used a Twitter scraper to retrieve the last 3000 tweets from every university in the sample (N=14). I then found a ratio of how many of the tweets contained one of the above keywords, putting the tweet into a category: "sports" or "academics" (or in rare cases, both).
